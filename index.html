About us

Welcome to BPS MUN 2025, the flagship Model United Nations conference of Vidya Niketan Birla Public School, Pilani. A platform where diplomacy meets intellect, BPS MUN is designed to cultivate critical thinking, negotiation skills, and global awareness among young minds. With a legacy of excellence, our conference brings together passionate delegates, experienced executive board members, and engaging committees that simulate real-world international affairs. BPS MUN 2025 features dynamic debates, well-researched agendas, and a professional MUN experience that challenges participants to think beyond borders. We emphasize comprehensive research, diplomatic courtesy, and strategic problem-solving to prepare delegates for leadership roles in the future.

Committies

BPS MUN 2025 presents a dynamic range of committees, each offering a platform for intense debate, strategic negotiations, and impactful policymaking. Delegates will engage in discussions across UNHRC, UNGA, UNSC, WHO, UNESCO, AIPPM, Crisis Committee, and IPC, tackling contemporary global challenges, national political discourses, and high-intensity crisis simulations. Whether shaping international policies, defending national interests, or resolving futuristic dilemmas, each committee demands diplomacy, leadership, and critical thinking.
